Recovery Suggestions

A diet for recovery should include: Complex carbohydrates (50% to 55% of the calories you consume), which means plenty of grains, fruits and vegetable Dairy products or other foods rich in calcium (calcium-fortified beverages, tofu, kale), two to three cups per day Moderate protein (15% to 20% of calories): two to four ounces twice a day
